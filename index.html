Welcome to DG Veterinary Clinic

Your Trusted Partner for Pet Health and Wellness

DG Veterinary Clinic is a renowned animal care center located in Selaiyur, Chennai, dedicated to providing exceptional medical care for your beloved pets.

We offer specialized services including minor surgeries, breast cancer treatment, gynecology, and general consultation. Our clinic is equipped to handle emergencies 24/7, ensuring your pet gets the best care whenever needed.

In addition to medical services, we also provide high-quality dog food and nutritional advice to keep your pets healthy and active.
